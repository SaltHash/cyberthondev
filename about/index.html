Our Mission

We are a nonprofit organization with a mission to bring cybersecurity to all. Our staff believe that everyone should have access to adequate technological education. Join us in our mission by taking free classes in our discord server and showing up to our cybersecurity and programming competitions.

Past Competition Images

See what previous competitions actually looked like

These pictures were taken during the very first CyberThon competition, which took place in March 2025. This exciting new event brought together tech enthusiasts, students, and problem solvers from various backgrounds to compete in a fast-paced and engaging cybersecurity challenge. Each participating team was given four hours to earn as many points as possible by completing a variety of tasks, each designed with different levels of difficulty to test their technical knowledge, strategic thinking, and teamwork.

The competition featured a diverse set of challenges that ranged from beginner-friendly puzzles to complex real-world inspired scenarios. Teams had to adapt quickly, collaborate effectively, and manage their time wisely in order to climb the leaderboard. The sense of urgency and the thrill of solving tough problems under pressure made the experience both challenging and incredibly rewarding.

You can find the full records and scores from this exciting event in our Discord, located in the competitions section. There, you'll also find highlights, discussions, and insights from participants and staff members who made the event such a success.

We plan to host many more fun events like this, join our discord and stick around to figure out our next project!
